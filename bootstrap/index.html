Kamen Rider Zero-One

Kamen Rider Zero-One (仮面ライダーゼロワン, Kamen Raidā Zerowan) is a Japanese tokusatsu drama in Toei Company's Kamen Rider Series. It is the first series to debut during the Reiwa period and the thirty-fourth overall. The series premiered on September 1, 2019 and joined Kishiryu Sentai Ryusoulger in the Super Hero Time line-up after the finale of Kamen Rider Zi-O. After Ryusoulger concluded, the series was joined by Mashin Sentai Kiramager on March 8, 2020. After the finale of Zero-One, Kiramager was joined by Kamen Rider Saber in the Super Hero Time block.
